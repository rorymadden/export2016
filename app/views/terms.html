RIGHT OF ACCESS

A processing fee of €6.35 will be charged and we will respond to your request within 40 calendar days. Included in the request should be a copy of your driver’s licence or passport and accompanied by a check or bankers draft for €6.35.

Under section 6 of the Acts, you have a right to have your personal data corrected, if inaccurate, or erased, in the case that we do not have a legitimate reason for retaining the data.
